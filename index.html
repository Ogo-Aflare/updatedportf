My Story

I Started with a Voice.
Then I Learned to
Design with It.

Before I ever opened Photoshop, I was on a stage — singing, worshipping, performing. Music was my first language. It taught me something that no design school can fully teach: how to make people feel something.

"Emotion is the most powerful branding tool in the world."

When I transitioned into graphic design and branding, I didn't leave music behind — I carried it into every pixel, every color choice, every campaign. I began to see design not as decoration, but as communication at its most powerful form.

Today, I operate at the intersection of design, media, and music. I've managed social media pages that built communities, created visuals that stopped thumbs mid-scroll, directed musical productions, and built brand identities that clients are proud to carry. What I offer isn't a service — it's a creative partnership.

Behind the Results

01
Social Media Growth for a Local Brand
Social Media Management · Content Strategy
+340% Reach in 90 Days
Problem
A growing local business had a strong product but almost no social media presence. Their posts were inconsistent, unbranded, and getting low engagement — essentially invisible online.
Strategy
Rebuilt their visual identity for social platforms. Created a monthly content calendar mixing educational posts, product highlights, behind-the-scenes, and community content to build authentic connection.
Execution
Designed all graphics in-house, wrote captions with brand voice, scheduled posts consistently 5x per week, engaged with comments, and ran one targeted content push per month.
Result
340% increase in organic reach within 3 months. Follower count grew 4×. Business began receiving direct inquiries via DMs. The brand's social became their top lead generation channel.
02
Brand Identity Redesign
Branding · Visual Design · Strategy
Complete Brand Overhaul
Problem
An organization had been operating for years with an outdated identity that no longer reflected their growth, values, or target audience. They looked smaller than they were.
Strategy
Conducted a brand audit. Identified core values, audience expectations, and competitors. Developed a new visual direction that communicated professionalism, warmth, and forward vision.
Execution
Delivered a new logo, full color palette, typography system, brand voice guide, business card, letterhead, and digital template pack — all cohesive and rollout-ready.
Result
The client reported immediate positive feedback from stakeholders and partners. New brand materials doubled perceived value at events and meetings. Recruitment inquiries increased noticeably post-launch.
03
Vox Pulchra — Music & Visual Brand
Music Direction · Branding · Content Creation
Community Built from Scratch
Problem
Vox Pulchra needed to grow from a small singing group into a recognizable music brand with a distinct identity, consistent visual presence, and growing digital audience.
Strategy
Unified the music direction with a strong visual brand. Developed a content strategy around worship sessions, rehearsal clips, and behind-the-scenes moments to create emotional connection with audiences.
Execution
Directed live recording sessions, designed all promotional materials, managed social media pages, and personally led the choir in vocal development — blending creative direction with hands-on performance.
Result
Vox Pulchra grew a loyal community of followers, received invitations to minister at events, and became known as a premium worship experience brand — recognized visually and sonically.
